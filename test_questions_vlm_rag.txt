Suggested document QA questions (edit based on your own documents):

1. What is the publication date shown in the document?
2. Who is the author or organization responsible for this report?
3. What is the main objective stated in the executive summary?
4. Which section discusses methodology?
5. What dataset size is reported?
6. What is the top-line result shown in the summary chart?
7. Which figure reports the highest value, and what is that value?
8. What limitation is explicitly mentioned by the authors?
9. What recommendation is given in the conclusion section?
10. What does Table 1 compare?
11. What is the unit used on the y-axis of the main chart?
12. Which year has the lowest metric in the trend figure?
13. What assumption is made before the experiment?
14. What baseline method is used for comparison?
15. What performance metric improves the most?
16. Is there any contradictory statement across sections? If yes, where?
17. Which appendix contains implementation details?
18. What threshold value is used in the analysis?
19. What future work is suggested?
20. Provide one claim from the report and cite its page/figure source.

Labeling guide:
- Correct: factually supported by source
- Partial: partly correct or incomplete
- Hallucinated: unsupported or incorrect

Error type examples:
- retrieval miss
- OCR noise
- wrong grounding
- overconfident generation
- ambiguous question
